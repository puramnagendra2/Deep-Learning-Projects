Bacterial Spot

Bacterial Spot is a common disease that causes significant damage to leaves, stems, and fruit. It is caused by bacteria of the genus Xanthomonas, and can be particularly devastating in warm, humid conditions.


Symptoms

  • Small, water-soaked spots appear on the leaves, which soon turn dark brown or black. These spots may be surrounded by a yellow halo. In severe cases, the spots may merge, causing the leaves to wither and die.
  • Dark brown or black lesions may develop on the stems.
  • Small, raised spots appear on the fruit, which may become sunken and scabby as they develop.
